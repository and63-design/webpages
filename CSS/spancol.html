Professional Word Document

To make your document look professionally produced, Word provides header, footer, cover page, and text box designs that complement each other. You can add a matching cover page, header, and sidebar. Click insert and then choose the element you want from the different galleries.

Coordinated Document Styles

Themes and styles also help keep your document Coordinated. When you click Design and choose a new Theme, the pictures, charts, and SmartArt graphics change to match your new theme. When you apply styles, your headings change to match the new theme.
